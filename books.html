Books Read

I never really had a habit of reading novels. Its not like I didn't like to read novels, but I just couldn't because everytime I tried to read one, no matter how interesting it was, I fall straight asleep after reading 10 pages! But I always favoured reading because all my friends who had a habit of reading were great in english. So, it seemed to me that reading improves your english. Well, why wouldn't it?

So, I took this resolution in 2015 to start reading novels. It was my new year resolution! And there started my reading habit. It was difficult in the beginning. I used to listen to the audiobook version of the novel and simultaneously read the PDF version of the novel. My first novel was Eragon, of the Inheritance Cycle by Christopher Paolini. I enjoyed it because it was my type of novel, a fantasy one.


Books Read in 2015

“A book is a dream that you hold in your hand.” - Neil Gaiman



Book - Eragon

Author - Christopher Paolini

Review - My first novel. Read it with the help of an audiobook from the Listening Library. Although I did see the Eragon movie before reading this, I was told that the movie was rubbish, and yes, after reading the book I realized just how much he skipped and assumed things in the movie.



Book - Eldest

Author - Christopher Paolini

Review - The second book of the Inheritance Cycle. This book is a little boring as there is a lot of travelling. But it gets interesting as the story progresses and has a pretty good ending.



Book - Brisingr

Author - Christopher Paolini

Review - The third book of the Inheritance Cycle. This book is a much more interesting as compared to the first two books.



Book - Inheritance

Author - Christopher Paolini

Review - The fourth and the last book of the Inheritance Cycle. This book is good but I expected it to be better. Most of the story is fine but I was a little disappointed with the ending.



Book - The Present

Author - Spencer Johnson

Review - I was told Spencer Johnson is very good with short stories. This book wasn't particulary interesting, but it was good.



Book - The Blue Umbrella

Author - Ruskin Bond

Review - I might have liked it if I read this when I was 5 years old. But reading it when I was much older made this book sound very childish.

Book - A Quiver Full of Arrows
