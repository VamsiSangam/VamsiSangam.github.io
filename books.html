Books Read

I never really had a habit of reading novels. Its not like I didn't like to read novels, but I just couldn't because everytime I tried to read one, no matter how interesting it was, I fall straight asleep after reading 10 pages! But I always favoured reading because all my friends who had a habit of reading were great in english. So, it seemed to me that reading improves your english. Well, why wouldn't it?

So, I took this resolution in 2015 to start reading novels. It was my new year resolution! And there started my reading habit. It was difficult in the beginning. I used to listen to the audiobook version of the novel and simultaneously read the PDF version of the novel. My first novel was Eragon, of the Inheritance Cycle by Christopher Paolini. I enjoyed it because it was my type of novel, a fantasy one.
